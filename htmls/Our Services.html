THE PHOTOVOLTAIC SYSTEM

HOW AND WHAT TO DO

To obtain reliable results it is necessary that the diagnosis activity is carried out independently by the person who built the photovoltaic system. The main diagnostic activities carried out by PV Service are:
  • Analysis of the As-Built Project documentation
  • Visual Analysis for a Verification of Correspondence with the standards of the Construction regulations, to identify any obvious problems .
  • Deterioration analysis:
    1. Inspection of the integrity of the surface and the frame of the PV modules
    2. Control of PV modules that show visual alterations
    3. Visual inspection of the roof anchoring structure
    4. Control of the wiring if they are up to standard and still efficient or if they present dispersion
  • Instrumental Analysis
    1. Technical testing
    2. Thermography
    3. Verification of the PR
    4. Verification of the IV curve
